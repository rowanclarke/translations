Sveto Pismo ili Biblija

The Holy Bible in Serbian (Latin alphabet), translated by Vuk Karadžić and Đura Daničić

Public Domain
Language: srpski jezik (Serbian)
Translation by: Vuk Karadžić, Đura Daničić

Vuk Karadžić translated the New Testament of the Bible in Serbian, publishing it in Vienna in 1847. Đura Daničić assisted Karadžić in that work, and later added an Old Testament translation in 1867. The whole Bible was published in 1868. This copy was brought to you by eBible.org.
